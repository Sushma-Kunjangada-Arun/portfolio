About Me

I started my tech journey in Bangalore, working as an Integration Developer at Happiest Minds Technologies. For over two years, I helped enterprise clients migrate and modernize their systems — improving performance, building APIs, and collaborating closely with QA and backend teams. It gave me a solid foundation in real-world software development and taught me how to think critically under pressure.

Wanting to go beyond backend systems and understand the full product — from how it looks to how users interact with it — I pursued a Master’s in Information Systems at Northeastern University. Since then, I’ve built full stack projects like a multilingual youth support platform and an iOS budgeting app powered by speech recognition and machine learning.

I’ve worked with React, Node.js, Swift, TypeScript, MongoDB, and SQL, and I enjoy picking up new tools as projects demand. I care about writing clean code, creating smooth user experiences, and building software that’s actually useful. I love collaborating, learning through feedback, and staying curious about what’s next.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me reading novels, getting lost in comics, binge-watching great series, or experimenting in the kitchen. I’m also a big fan of staying organized and appreciate good design — especially the little details that make a big difference. I'm always up for a good project, a new challenge, or working with people excited about what they’re building.
